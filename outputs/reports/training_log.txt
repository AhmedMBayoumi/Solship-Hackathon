SECTION 4 — FEATURE ENGINEERING LOG
================================================================================

v1 — Initial feature set (created with processed CSV):
  - lag_1     : load 15 min ago
  - lag_4     : load 1 hour ago
  - lag_96    : load 24 hours ago
  - lag_192   : load 48 hours ago
  - lag_672   : load 1 week ago
  - hour      : hour of day (0-23)
  - dow       : day of week (0=Monday)
  - month     : month (1-12)
  - tariff_band : F1/F2/F3 (one-hot or label encoded)
  - is_holiday  : 0/1 Italian national holiday
  - pv_kw     : contemporaneous PV generation (known from forecast or actuals)

Candidates to add:
  - Temperature (Sondrio) from Open-Meteo (free, no API key)
  - Solar irradiance from Open-Meteo (improves PV forecast, indirectly load)
  - Rolling mean load (7-day, 1-day)
  - sin/cos encoding of hour and month (cyclical)

================================================================================
SECTION 5 — KEY NUMBERS TO REMEMBER
================================================================================

Battery:
  Capacity      : 16 kWh
  Power limit   : ±8 kW (charge/discharge)
  Grid limit    : ±6 kW (import/export)
  Efficiency    : √0.90 each direction (round-trip ≈ 90%)
  SOC init      : 8.0 kWh (50%) at start of each month

Tariff (buy price, Italian TOU):
  F1 (peak)     : EUR 0.2540/kWh — Mon-Fri 08:00-19:00 (non-holiday)
  F2 (mid)      : EUR 0.2682/kWh — Mon-Fri 07-08 & 19-23, Sat 07-23 (non-holiday)
  F3 (off-peak) : EUR 0.2440/kWh — nights (<07:00 & ≥23:00), Sundays, holidays
  Sell price    : dynamic market price (Selling_price_eur_kwh column)

Dataset:
  Training      : 2024 full year (35,040 rows at 15-min resolution)
  Test          : April 2025 + September 2025
  Timestep      : 15 min → multiply kW × 0.25 for kWh energy

Easter Monday 2025: April 21 → F3 all day (cheap charging opportunity)
